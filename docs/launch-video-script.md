# Launch Video Script

## Purpose

Use this script for a short launch video on your site, social post, product hunt style page, or founder walkthrough.

This version is tighter and more spoken than the live demo script. It is designed to sound natural on camera.

## Recommended length

- Short version: 60 to 90 seconds
- Standard version: 2 to 3 minutes

## 90-Second Version

### Opening

Say this:

> If you are shipping AI, one of the hardest questions is not just what laws exist. It is which ones actually apply to your product, what you need to do next, and what proof you should already have ready.

### Problem

Say this:

> Most startups and SMBs do not need a giant governance platform on day one. They need a way to get clarity fast, turn that into action, and stay current as the product changes.

### Product intro

Say this:

> That is what Spanforge Compass does. You describe your AI system once, Compass maps the most relevant laws, shows your top obligations, and turns the output into owner-ready actions and evidence guidance.

### Workflow

Say this while showing the product:

> Start with the assessment. You can use a preset or just describe your product in plain English.

> Then review the results. Compass gives you a law map, a prioritized action plan, and the next evidence your team should collect.

> From there, use the evidence workspace to attach proof, fill gaps, and keep the work reusable for launch reviews, procurement, or customer diligence.

### Repeat value

Say this:

> And when laws change, your product expands, or evidence goes stale, Compass helps you see what changed so you do not have to rebuild everything from scratch.

### Close

Say this:

> If your team is shipping AI and needs to understand what laws matter, what to do next, and what proof to keep ready, that is exactly what Compass is built for.

## 2-3 Minute Version

### Opening hook

> Shipping AI is not just a product problem anymore. It is also a readiness problem. Founders, product teams, and compliance operators are being asked the same questions again and again: which laws apply, what obligations are in scope, and what evidence can we actually show?

### Why the current approach breaks

> For small teams, the usual options are bad. Either you piece things together manually from legal updates and internal docs, or you try to jump straight into heavyweight compliance tooling that is built for much larger organizations.

### What Compass is

> Spanforge Compass is built for the middle ground. It helps a small team understand applicability, turn that into action and evidence, and stay current over time.

### Walkthrough

> You start by describing the AI product. Compass supports presets, guided input, and a quick-start path in plain English.

> From there, the system produces a concrete law map, highlights key obligations, and generates an action plan with suggested owners, timing, and evidence guidance.

> The evidence workspace then helps the team collect and organize proof, including recommended artifacts and quick capture for links, policies, and internal notes.

> Finally, the dashboard and alerts workflow make the product useful beyond day one by showing drift, legal updates, stale evidence, and current priorities.

### Audience fit

> This is especially useful for founders preparing for launch or procurement, product leads expanding AI features, compliance generalists, and fractional advisors supporting startups.

### Final close

> Compass is not trying to replace legal advice or become a heavyweight GRC suite. It is designed to help a smaller team move from uncertainty to readiness with a workflow they can actually use.

## Suggested shot order

1. Homepage promise
2. Assessment quick-start
3. Results action plan
4. Evidence workspace
5. Dashboard or alerts
6. Final logo or CTA

## Suggested CTA lines

Use one of these at the end:

- Run your first AI law assessment.
- See which laws apply before launch or diligence blocks you.
- Turn your AI compliance questions into actions and evidence.
